I aim to develop robotic systems that are trustworthy, adaptive, and socially aware for environments such as healthcare and education. My work blends human-robot interaction (HRI), spatial reasoning, and AI ethics.

Research Focus

Human-Robot Interaction: I study the factors that help people trust robots, using cues like gaze, motion, and speech to help systems infer user needs and adapt their behavior.

Socially-Aware Navigation: I aim to design motion strategies that respect social norms and personal space (proxemics), making robot movement more trustworthy.
